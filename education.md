This background proved vital during the Winter 2026 term, where I served as the front-end developer for an 8-week Software Methodology project. My team built a comprehensive scheduler for the university’s Multicultural Center (MCC), enabling over 30 clubs to manage events and export data via ICS links. By blending the collaborative communication skills I honed through years of pre-professional dance training with my technical expertise, I helped navigate the complexities of group choreography and software development to deliver a functional, user-centric solution for the campus community.
